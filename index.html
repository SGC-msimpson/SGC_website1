Welcome

Welcome to the Skin Genetics Consortium website. We are a collaborative network of researchers, clinicians, and scientists dedicated to advancing the field of dermatogenetics.

About Us

The Skin Genetics Consortium brings together experts from around the world to study the genetic factors that affect skin health and disease. Our mission is to facilitate groundbreaking research that leads to improved diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of skin conditions.

Our Research

Our research focuses on understanding the genetic basis of skin disorders, identifying biomarkers, and developing innovative therapeutic approaches. Key areas of research include:

  • Genetic markers of skin diseases
  • Genomic studies in diverse populations
  • Innovative treatments and personalized medicine
