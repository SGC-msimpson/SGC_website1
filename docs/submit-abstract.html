ESDR 2026 Satellite Event

Submit an abstract to apply for a travel award

We are offering slots to pre-doctoral and post-doctoral researchers to give a 5 minute lightning talk at the symposium. Selected speakers will be offered a travel award for attendance at the meeting.

Europe
Free ESDR registration + €1000 for hotel & travel
Outside Europe
Free ESDR registration + €2000 for hotel & travel
Travel awards are funded by the LEO Foundation and ESDR

Deadline: 30/04/26
To apply for an award please fill in the form before the deadline. We will inform successful travel awardees by end of June, prior to ESDR early registration closure.

(NB - this is separate from the ESDR main programme abstract submission and you are welcome to submit to both)

Submission Guidelines

  • This is the abstract submission form for the Large-Scale Genetics to Cellular Mechanisms of Skin Disease (G2CM) ESDR satellite event.
  • Presenting author must be a pre-doctoral or post-doctoral researcher.
  • Abstracts must be submitted in English.
  • Abstract: maximum 2,300 characters including spaces.
  • Career stage description: maximum 150 words.
  • Deadline: 30th April 2026.
  • Accepted abstracts will be notified by email.
